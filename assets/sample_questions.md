# Sample Questions for Farmers - Crop Advisory, Weather Forecast, and Pests & Diseases

This document contains sample questions that farmers can ask about crop advisory, weather forecast, and pests & diseases in both English and Hindi.

---

## 1. Crop Advisory Questions

### English

#### Crop Selection & Planning
- Which crops are best suited for my region?
- What crops can I grow in the current season?
- Which crop variety gives the best yield?
- What is the best time to sow wheat?
- When should I plant rice in my area?
- Which crops are suitable for rainfed conditions?
- What crops can I grow in summer?
- Which crops require less water?
- What is the best crop rotation for my field?
- Which crops are profitable this year?

#### Seed & Sowing
- What is the recommended seed rate for wheat?
- How much seed do I need per acre for rice?
- What spacing should I follow for cotton?
- When is the best time to sow groundnut?
- How deep should I sow maize seeds?
- What is the ideal seed spacing for soybean?
- Which seed variety is best for my area?
- How to prepare seeds before sowing?
- What is the germination rate for this variety?
- Should I use treated seeds?

#### Fertilizer & Nutrition
- How much fertilizer does wheat need?
- What fertilizer should I apply to rice?

- How much DAP should I use per acre?
- What is the recommended fertilizer dose for cotton?
- When to apply top dressing fertilizer?
- What organic fertilizers can I use?
- How to apply fertilizer to my crop?
- What micronutrients does my crop need?
- How much compost should I add?

#### Irrigation & Water Management
- How often should I irrigate wheat?
- When should I irrigate rice fields?
- How much water does cotton need?
- What is the best irrigation method for my crop?
- When to stop irrigation before harvest?
- How to manage water during drought?
- What is the critical irrigation stage for maize?
- How to check if my crop needs water?
- What is the water requirement for soybean?
- How to save water in farming?

#### Crop Growth & Management
- How long does wheat take to mature?
- What is the growth stages of rice?
- When should I thin my crop?
- How to manage weeds in my field?
- What is the best time for weeding?
- How to improve crop yield?
- What is intercropping and how to do it?
- When should I harvest my crop?
- How to know if my crop is ready for harvest?
- What is the expected yield per acre?

#### Crop Care & Maintenance
- How to protect my crop from birds?
- What is the best way to store harvested crop?
- How to maintain soil health for next season?
- What cover crops can I grow?
- How to manage crop residues?
- What is mulching and how to do it?

- What is crop rotation and why is it important?
- How to prepare land for next crop?
- What is the best time for land preparation?
